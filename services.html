Job Integration Support

Navigating the Canadian job market can be challenging. We provide targeted assistance including:

  • **CV/Resume Workshops:** Tailoring your experience to Canadian standards.
  • **Cover Letter Guidance:** Crafting compelling letters for specific roles.
  • **Interview Preparation:** Practice sessions and tips for common tech/general interview questions.
  • **Company Information/Matching:** Providing resources and guidance on finding companies hiring in relevant tech fields in Canada (Note: Direct automated matching requires more complex systems; we focus on guidance and resources).
  • **Networking Strategies:** Tips for building professional connections in Canada.
